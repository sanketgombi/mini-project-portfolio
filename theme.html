Introduction

Our mini-project theme focuses on horticulture, a vital field that emphasizes the cultivation of fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants. Horticulture plays a crucial role in improving nutrition, beautifying surroundings, and enhancing quality of life.

This theme combines science, creativity, and sustainability, bringing technology and nature together to create efficient cultivation systems.

Through this project, we explore how engineering, automation, and smart technologies can enhance productivity and environmental balance.

Project Goals and Motivation

The goal of this project is to apply engineering and innovation to improve horticultural practices by reducing manual effort and optimizing resource utilization.

We aim to design sustainable, efficient, and scalable solutions that help increase productivity and promote eco-friendly cultivation methods.

By applying our technical knowledge, we strive to contribute to modern agriculture and sustainable development.
