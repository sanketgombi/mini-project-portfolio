Product Vision Board

A structured overview of the purpose, users, needs, solution, and business goals.

Purpose & Positive Change

Purpose: To simplify and standardize post-harvest flower sorting so farmers earn higher quality value.

Positive Change: Reduces manual labor dependency and ensures uniform quality for small farmers.

Target Group

  • Flower farmers & Vendors
  • Floriculture cooperatives
  • Small-scale horticulture units

Needs

  • Inconsistent quality sorting
  • High labor shortages
  • Post-harvest wastage

Product

  • Gentle mechanical handling
  • Low-cost automation
  • Modular rural design

Business Goals

  • Standardized grading profit
  • Early farmer adoption
  • Scalable production models
